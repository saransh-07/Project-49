Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry.
  2. A camel can walk easily on sand because of its specially designed feet.
  3. When a camel finds water it drinks as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana sub-order.
  2. Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail which they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
